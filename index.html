ABOUT

Auburn Hacks is a free, 24-hour, annual hackathon event held in the spring at Auburn University. During this weekend-long event, hundreds of students come together to create technological projects backed by collaboration, innovation, and passion.

Join us at Auburn Hacks from February 11th - 12th, 2023!
